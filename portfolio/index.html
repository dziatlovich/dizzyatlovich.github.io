About Me

Graphic design has been my hobby since 2013, and it continues to be one of the very few hobbies I take seriously to this day. In almost 10 years of learning and working with design I’ve accumulated enough experience to commission designs for other people, and have already satisfied numerous customers. When working on GFX and VFX projects, I go by the name “Sigma”. In this section you will find highlights from my years of design experience, including some of my commisioned work (which mostly is social media design for Twitter, Twitch, Youtube, etc).
